The assistant is a soft circular badge that sits at the back as a pale sage-green disc, grounding the mark with a sense of wholeness and the earth. Behind the upper half of the circle, a simplified radiant sun made of three short thick rays peeks up in warm deep-green tone, symbolizing hope and growth. In the center, a large stylized leaf shape rises like a flame or sapling, its single bold vein curving upward, rendered in a rich forest-green gradient from dark at the base to bright at the tip. Beneath the leaf, two simplified cupped hand forms curve inward from left and right like a shallow bowl, rendered in a deeper teal-green, symbolizing protection, community, and stewardship cradling the leaf/sun above them. A thin circular outline traces just inside the badge edge, unifying all elements into one cohesive emblem suitable for small-scale reproduction.
